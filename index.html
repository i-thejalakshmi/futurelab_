Skillbee Futurelab is a premier AI-driven educational hub based in Kottakkal, Malappuram, founded and operated entirely by AI Engineers. Unlike traditional academies, our classes are conducted by engineers who bring real-world technical depth to every session. At the heart of Kerala’s tech evolution, we aim to empower the next generation of innovators with the skills that truly matter.

We bridge the gap between theory and execution, making elite AI knowledge accessible to the local community.
